Our story

How Bio Shield began — and where we're headed

Bio Shield was founded on a simple yet powerful idea: that people shouldn’t have to choose between protecting themselves from insects and protecting the planet. The inspiration came when our founders noticed that most insect repellents on the market relied heavily on harsh chemicals, often posing risks to both human health and the environment. Driven by a passion for sustainability and wellness, they set out to develop a safer, eco-friendly alternative—one made with natural ingredients that would be as kind to the earth as it was effective against pests.

After months of research, testing, and refinement, Bio Shield introduced its first eco-friendly insect repellent. It quickly gained attention for its plant-based formula, which not only provided strong protection but also avoided the toxins found in traditional sprays. Families, outdoor enthusiasts, and environmentally conscious consumers embraced it, proving that eco-responsibility and performance could go hand in hand.

From that first breakthrough product, Bio Shield has continued to grow and innovate. The success of the original repellent paved the way for an expanded line of solutions—from household pest deterrents to skin-friendly sprays and pet-safe formulas. Today, Bio Shield remains committed to its mission of blending science, nature, and sustainability to create products that shield people—and the planet—from harm.

“We wanted to show the world that choosing green doesn’t mean compromising protection. That idea changed everything.” — Co-founder, Bio Shield
